Born October 1955, William Henry Gates, known as Bill Gates would become one of the richest and most influential men in the world. Raised in Seattle, Washington, Gates would found a company in his college years called Microsoft with his colleague Paul Allen. He is an entrepenuer, philanthropist as well as a software developer. With his work he has created one of the largest used computer operating systems in the world and has used his wealth to create the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation as a way to fund projects for world-wide development.

Melinda French Gates [3] (born Melinda Ann French; August 15, 1964) is an American philanthropist and former computer scientist and general manager at Microsoft. French Gates has consistently been ranked as one of the world's most powerful women by Forbes.[4] In 2000, she and her then-husband Bill Gates co-founded the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, the world's largest private charitable organization as of 2015. [5] She and her ex-husband have been awarded the US Presidential Medal of Freedom and the French Legion of Honour. In early May 2021, Bill and Melinda Gates announced they were getting divorced but will still remain co-chairs of the foundation.[6]
